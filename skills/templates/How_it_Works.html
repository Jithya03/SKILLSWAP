How SkillSwap Works

Learn and teach skills through a simple exchange system — no money involved.

1. Create a Profile

Sign up and list the skills you can teach and the skills you want to learn.

2. Find a Match

Browse available skills and connect with someone who matches your interests.

3. Swap Skills

Schedule sessions and exchange knowledge in a fair, skill-for-skill system.
